Standard Plan Details

The Standard Plan is designed for those new to fitness or who prefer a steady approach to their fitness journey. It offfers an introduction to nutrition and training.

  • Access to Basic Workout Plans: Start your fitness journey with a variety of foundational workout routines that cover all body parts and are designed for balanced development.
  • 2 Personal Training Sessions per Month: Receive personalized attention and coaching twice a month to ensure you are on the right track and performing exercises correctly.
  • Use of Gym Facilities: Enjoy full access to our state-of-the-art gym facilities, including cardio equipment, weight machines, and free weights during staffed hours.
  • 10% Discount on Supplements: Benefit from a 10% discount on high-quality nutritional supplements available at our facility to support your health and fitness goals.
  • Monthly Fitness Assessments: Track your progress with monthly assessments that include body measurements, fitness tests, and personalized feedback.
  • Access to Member-Only Events: Take part in exclusive events such as seminars, workshops, and social gatherings, which are reserved for members only.
  • Standard Support Response: Have your general inquiries answered within a standard timeframe, ensuring you have the support you need when you need it.
  • Weekly Newsletter: Stay informed and motivated with our weekly newsletter featuring fitness tips, nutritional advice, and the latest gym updates.

Professional Plan Details

The Professional Plan is designed for those that consider themselves at the intermediate fitness level. It offers more comprehensive nutritional and training protocols.

  • Customized Workout Plans: Experience workouts tailored to your individual goals and preferences, evolving with your progress for continued improvement.
  • 5 Personal Training Sessions per Month: Enhance your routine with five expert-led training sessions each month, focusing on advanced techniques for optimal results.
  • Nutritional Guidance and Meal Planning: Collaborate with nutrition experts to craft a meal plan that complements your workout regimen and dietary needs.
  • 15% Discount on Supplements and Gear: Enjoy exclusive savings on our premium range of supplements and fitness gear to boost your performance and recovery.
  • Bi-Weekly Fitness Assessments: Keep track of your fitness journey with detailed bi-weekly assessments to measure progress and fine-tune your program.
  • Priority Booking for Classes: Secure your spot in high-demand classes with priority booking, ensuring you never miss your favorite sessions.
  • Access to Advanced Classes: Challenge yourself with access to advanced-level classes, designed to push your limits and enhance your abilities.
  • 24/7 Gym Access: Enjoy the flexibility to train at your convenience with around-the-clock access to the gym, fitting your fitness into any schedule.
  • Priority Support Response: Receive prompt and attentive service for all your inquiries, giving you the timely assistance you need.

Master Plan Details

The Master Plan is designed for those that are experienced in their fitness level and wish to compete. It offers aggressive nutritional and training protocols to prepare the individual for contests.

  • All-Access Workout Plans: Gain unlimited access to a wide range of workout plans, each designed to target different fitness goals and preferences.
  • Unlimited Personal Training Sessions: Benefit from unlimited one-on-one sessions with our certified trainers, providing personalized guidance every step of the way.
  • Full Nutritional Program with Custom Meals: Receive a comprehensive nutritional program, including custom meal plans tailored to your specific dietary requirements and fitness objectives.
  • 20% Discount on All Products and Services: Enjoy an exclusive 20% discount on all products and services offered at our facility, including merchandise, supplements, and additional classes.
  • Weekly One-on-One Fitness Assessments: Stay on track with weekly personal assessments to monitor your progress, adjust your plan, and set new goals.
  • Complimentary Massage and Spa Services: Relax and rejuvenate with complimentary access to our massage and spa services, helping you recover and unwind after your workouts.
  • Dedicated Support and Consultation: Receive dedicated support and consultation from our team, ensuring you have expert advice and assistance whenever needed.
