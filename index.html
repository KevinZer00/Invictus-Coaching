Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cancellation policy? +
You can cancel your membership at any time with a 30-day notice. No cancellation fees apply.
Do you offer group fitness classes? +
Yes, we offer a variety of group fitness classes, including yoga, pilates, spinning, and more. Check our class schedule for details.
Can I pause my membership? +
Yes, you can pause your membership for a minimum of one month and a maximum of six months. A pause fee may apply.
Are personal training sessions included in my membership? +
Personal training sessions are not included in the standard membership plans but can be purchased separately. Some premium plans may include personal training sessions.
What amenities are available at the gym? +
Our facilities include state-of-the-art fitness equipment, locker rooms, showers, a sauna, and a juice bar. Premium members have access to additional amenities.
Is there a trial membership available? +
Yes, we offer a 7-day trial membership for new clients. This trial includes access to the gym facilities and group fitness classes.
